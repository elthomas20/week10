Mummy's Mask

Welcome to The Mithral Tabletop! We are just a group of friends making a podcast together using our favorite TTRPG: Pathfinder 2e. In our first ever podcast, we will be taking the adventure path "Mummy's Mask" created by PaizoInc and converting it to Pathfinder 2e, thanks to the ingenuity of our GM, Andrew Wahba. We are so excited to share our rendition of this Ancient Egyptian-esque tale. Follow our adventurers as we delve deep into the mysteries of Ancient Osirion!
